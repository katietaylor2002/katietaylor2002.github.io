About Me.

I am a first-year undergraduate at the University of Bath studying Computer Science. I am proficient in programming languages such as Python, Java, C, VB.net, PHP, SQL and HTML. I am a member of the Bath Computer Science Society and the Women in Technology society.

In my spare time I am a keen musician. I play the cello, tenor saxophone and sing. I take part in numerous performances and concerts in a semester as well as being ensemble manager for the orchestra. I also am a social tennis player and archer.
